If a creature communicates telepathically with the Scion of Nidhogg, the Scion of Nidhogg learns the creature's greatest desires if the Scion of Nidhogg can see the creature.

Tail.

Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10ft. one target. Hit: 15 (3d6 + 5) bludgeoning damage.

Enslave (3/ day)

The Scion of Nidhogg targets one creature it can see within 30 feet of it. The target must succeed on a DC 13 wisdom saving throw or be magically chargmed by the Scion of Nidhogg until the Scion of Nidhogg dies or until it is on a different plane of existence from the target. The charmed target is under the Scion of Nidhogg's control and can't take reactions, and the Scion of Nidhogg and the taret can communicate telepathically with each other over any distance.

Whenever the charmed target takes damage, the target can repeat the saving throw. On a success, the effect ends. No more than once every 24 hours, the target can also repeate the saving throw when it is at least 1 mile from the Scion of Nidhogg.

Legendary Actions

The Scion of Nidhogg can take 2 legendary actions, choosing form the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Scion of Nidhogg regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.

Tail Swipe - See pass/fail gate 3

The Scion of Nidhogg can make one tail attack

Teleport (2 actions)- See pass/fail gate 3

The Scion of Nidhogg opens a hole into space and time and magically teleports, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, up to 15 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.

Psychic Drain (3 actions)

One creature charmed by the scion takes 10 (3d6) psychic damage and the aboleth regains hit points equal to the damage the creature takes.
